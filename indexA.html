Message from the Founder

I am Dr. Moussa Ousmane MINTA, founder and promoter of this institution, born from a deep passion for education and an unwavering commitment to the future of our children.

A journey marked by perseverance, my academic and professional path reflects a constant quest for excellence and dedication to teaching. Educated in the Malian school system from primary to university, I later pursued higher studies in the United States, where I earned a Master of Education (M.Ed.) at the prestigious NorthCentral University in Arizona.

The challenges were numerous. To finance my studies, I worked tirelessly, alternating between deliveries and restaurant jobs. These trials strengthened my determination to transform education in Africa, particularly in Mali.

Upon my return to Mali, I completed my academic journey with a Doctorate in Applied Linguistics (Bilingual Education) at the University Pedagogy Institute (IPU, formerly ISFRA), further consolidating my expertise in preschool, primary, secondary, and higher education systems.

A Committed Vision for Education: my return to Mali reflects a firm will to contribute to the improvement of the national education system and, by extension, to the training of the continent’s future leaders. Drawing on my international experience and pedagogical skills, I strive to offer high-quality education focused on innovation and equity.

One of my top priorities is access to education for disadvantaged groups. That is why our school complex is committed to offering free education to many children from underprivileged families; because I firmly believe that education is a right, not a privilege.

Our Mission: Through this institution, we cultivate academic excellence, civic values, and bilingualism, in order to prepare a generation capable of meeting both local and global challenges. Together, let us build the future!

With gratitude,
Dr. Moussa Ousmane MINTA
Founder & Promoter

Nos Activités Récentes

Préparation de la Rentrée Scolaire

À l’École Espoirs d’Afrique, la préparation de la rentrée est un moment de partage, de planification et d’unité entre le personnel enseignant et administratif. Ces moments sont essentiels pour garantir une année scolaire réussie, axée sur la qualité de l’enseignement et le bien-être des élèves.

Visite de Collaboration et Partenariat

Une délégation du Complexe Scolaire Espoirs d’Afrique a effectué une visite amicale auprès de deux écoles partenaires : le Complexe Scolaire Mamby Keïta et le Complexe Scolaire Nouveau Regard de N’Golobougou. Ce moment d’échanges et de fraternité a permis de renforcer les liens éducatifs et de promouvoir la collaboration entre institutions.
